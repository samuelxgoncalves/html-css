    num = int(input('Type a number: '))

Simple quotation

As Uenoyama said to Mafuyu: Would you stop looking like the whole world ended over a broken string?! Now I feel bad, like I got your hopes up just so they could crash back down! Gimme back my naptime, damn it!

This quote can be done by using <q> and <⁄q> tags. They wrap the quote in quotation marks, giving it a semantic meaning.

Quotation

According to Stepthen Corda in Introduction to Aerospace Engineering with a Flight Test Perspective:

As an aerospace engineer, you have the opportunity to contribute to the first flight of a new aircraft, a new spacecraft, or a new technology.

A block of quote can be done using, as the name would suggest, <blockquote> and <⁄blockquote> tags. This tag gives the option to use the parameter 'cite' to specify the source of the quotation (usually a URL).
