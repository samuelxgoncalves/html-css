Paragraphs and line breaks

You can write a paragraph in any way you want. You just have to write it inside <p> and </p> tags.

If you need to break a line in a specific place
- like here -
you can use the <br> tag.

Let's add some special symbols:

With '&reg;', '&copy;' and '&trade;' we can add to the website the symbols: ®, © and ™, respectively.

A few other examples include: € (euro), £ (pound), $ (dollar), ¥ (yen) and ¢ (american cent).

Other special symbols like ↑ (uparrow or uarr), ↓ (downarrow or darr), ← (leftarrow or larr), → (rightarrow or rarr) can also be added to webpages.
